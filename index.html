What We Do
Home Furnishing

Furnishing Home with Household furniture such as chairs, tables, sofas, cupboard, office Chairs & Tables, Bedframes, Kitchen Cabinet and Dining Table and chairs for equipping a house with the best Finishes & Fittings.

Interior Painting

Interior painting is application of paints in the interior portion of a  building. Paints majorly contribute to the mood and tone of your interior and the surfaces to be painted with different color and design of your choice.

Revamp & Repair of furniture

It can mean reupholstering, refinishing, restoring, refurbishing, renovating and adding a different look or design. Sometimes, details that deemed excessive or outdated are removed and new features are added to beautify your furniture.

Interior Decoration

Interior decoration is the art of decorating a residential home or commercial business according to a client's personal preferences and style. It includes selecting color schemes, flooring materials, curtain fixing, artwork and 3D Wallpaper, and Window accessories.
